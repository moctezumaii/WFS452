Irrigation and Fertilizer

  1. Identify the design: Split plot -design

  2. Identify the main plot and subplot factors (if applicable): main plot is the irrigation method and subplot is the fertilizer type

  3. Identify the experimental and observational unit for each factor: For irrigation method, the experimental unit is the field (because its applied to the whole field) and the observational unit is the plot. For the fertilizer type, the experimental unit and the observational unit are both the plot within the field.

  4. Write either:

    1. A linear model equation or

    2. An ANOVA model equation:

    \[ Y_{ijk}+\mu+\alpha_i+\beta_{ij}+\gamma_k+(\alpha\gamma)_{ik} +\epsilon_{ijk} \]

Where mu is the overall mean, alpha_i is the effect of irrigation method i, beta_ji is a random error due to field j nested in irrigation i (main plot error), gamma_k is the effect of fertilizer k, alpha x gamma_ik is the interaction between irrigation and fertilizer, and epsilon_ijk is the random subplot error.

Chick Feed and Temperature

  1. Identify the design: split plot design

  2. Identify the main plot and subplot factors (if applicable): The main plot is the feed type (standard vs. high-protein) applied to the whole pen, and the subplot factor is temperature (cool vs. warm) that is applied to cages within pens.

  3. Identify the experimental and observational unit for each factor: For feed type, the experimental unit is the pen, and the observation unit is the chicken. For temperature, the experimental unit is the cage within the pen, and the observational unit is the chicken (assuming based on the information given that we are weighing individual chickens).

  4. Write either:

    1. A linear model equation or

    2. An ANOVA model equation

      \[ Y_{ijk}=\mu+\alpha_i+\gamma_k+(\alpha\gamma)_ik+\epsilon_{ijk} \]

Where mu is the overall mean, alpha_i is the effect of feed type, beta_ji is a random effect of pen nested in feed type, gamma_k is the effect of temperature,, alpha x gamma_ik is the interaction between feed type and temperature, and epsilon_ijk is the subplot error.

Plant Genotypes

  1. Identify the design: This is a randomized complete block design.

  2. Identify the main plot and subplot factors (if applicable). N/A

  3. Identify the experimental and observational unit for each factor. The experimental unit for the genotype factor is the plot within a block, and its the same for observation unit since yield is recorded per plot.

  4. Write either:

    1. A linear model equation or

    2. An ANOVA model equation

      \[ Y_{ij} = \mu+\alpha_i+\beta_j+\epsilon_{ij} \]

Where mu is the overall mean, alpha_i is the fixed effect of genotype, beta_j is a random effect of block since there are soil differences, and epsilon_ij is the residual error term.
